My Biography

I am currently an independent academic & practitioner in the area of quantitative finance. Formerly, I was a Senior Economics Researcher at the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation (OIC) Ankara Centre, known as SESRIC. I am also an associate member of Global Association of Risk Professionals (GARP) and Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM), and regularly referee for a number of academic journals, including the Journal of Risk Finance, the Journal of Optimization, and Central European Journal of Operations Research. I hold a B.Eng. in Management Engineering from Istanbul Technical University (Turkey), and both an M.Sc. and a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics (Financial Mathematics) from the Middle East Technical University (Turkey). I completed my Master's and Doctoral dissertation studies at the University of Ulm (Germany) and Imperial College London (UK), respectively.
